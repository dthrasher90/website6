About Me

Hello, my name is Drew Thrasher, thanks for stopping by. The picture to the right is my family. I am a husband, father, a JavaScript programmer and quite possibly a coffee addict… but that’s debatable. I am an aspiring web developer with full year of web programming experience. We currently reside in Fairbanks, AK and we are planning to move o Salt Lake City by the end of the year. As former resident of Las Vegas, NV, we are very familiar with Utah. We used to travle up to Provo for camping trips. We love the outdoors and all the adventure the Utah desert brings.

I have been programming for almost a year now and you can see some of the applications and languages I have used for them. Check out my blog section for regular updates.
